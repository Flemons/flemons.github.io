Defining an asset

The Q4 events product digitizes the in-person experience of the annual general meeting. This is a yearly event that allow shareholders to hear results. upcoming projects, vote, and ask questions.

In this project I focused on the Q&A functionality, one of the most crucial steps in the process. This period opens the floor for any shareholder to submit a question for the executive leadership team.

In this 3 week project I had to work outside the traditional box of research and design in order to validate the high stakes decision making within the tight deadline. The result was an extreme confidence boost with our largest clients, and a approval for using our platform to conduct there meetings.

Note: This project is an example of recent visual work and does not yet contain a full described process.
