Distracted Driving HUD Product

Interaction Design Capstone Project

Brief

Increased cell phone use has had a negative effect on driving habits of almost everyone, especially younger drivers who are still new to the activity.

This capstone will see the design and development of a Heads Up Display (HUD) aimed at young adults (16-24 years old), and helping them focus on the road, not their phone, while driving.

The goal of the HUD will be to limit phone use and in turn distractions, while the user is driving. Phone content will be limited to the HUD, as well as most interactions, keeping the user focused forward on the road.

Canada's Housing Crisis Visualization

Web based visualization of Canada's rising house costs

Brief

Due to an extreme rise in property value across Canada, home ownership is becoming more and more difficult for lower-income families.

Working with the Canadian Government's Innovation Hub, and utilizing animating concepts for web based applications, this project aimed to vosualize the issue the issue of rising housing costs from the perspective of the common middle-class family.

The final website demonstrates this issue with a timeline beginning in 1984 to 2016. This website placed important dates along the horizontally scrolling timeline, do evoke a nostalgic viewpoint, and bring more context to how quickly the market changed.
