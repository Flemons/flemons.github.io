Toyota Canada Website Redesign

Personal project

Web Design | Usability

This project sought to modernize Toyota Canada's (2016) website.

The website was outdated and difficult to use. The majority of the content was centralized and hidden in a myriad of menus, making it difficult to find even something simple.

The initial project was completed with a final high fidelity mockup of the homepage, along with a detailed explanation of my findings.

BONCI: A Better App For Finding Restaurants

UX/UI Design | Usability | User Research

The purpose of the project was to identify a market sector that needed improvement and innovation.

The app selection for restaurant browsing process were poor, leaving a large market segment that could be improved. Research was conducted for a user flow in which the app could be designed around.

The project was finalized with a mockup displaying the finalized interface, as well as a key-path scenario to show a potential users personal experience through the app.
