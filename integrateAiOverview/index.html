Background

integrate.ai is an artificial intelligence product that puts the power of AI in the hands of their users. A web application was constructed that enabled users to interact with, learn from, and dive deeper into our AI and how it safely interacts with their data.

The AI's function is auto-allocation of clients' customers to promotional campaigns the AI deems is most likely to convert someone to purchase a product.

At my start, there was no previously completed design work of any kind, it was very much just an idea. This project undertook 2 years of direction changes and iterations, enabling me to head up projects in: product design, UI design, motion design, user research, design team development, and even some front-end development.
